If you use a personal health device with your iPhone (such as a Apple Watch) and use iOS8 or later (which includes Apple HealthKit), you can choose to include the data from the device in the study. You can set our app to get all, some, or none of the data. You can choose not to do this and still participate in the study.

We will NOT access your personal contacts, other applications, phone use habits, text messages, personal photos, or websites visited.
